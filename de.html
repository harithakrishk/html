The University of Calicut also known as Calicut University, is a multidisciplinary affiliating collegiate state (public) university near Kozhikode in northern Kerala, India. Established in 1968, it is the first university to be set up in northern Kerala. The university is co-ordinatinated by the University Grants Commission (re-accredited by NAAC with 'A' grade).[3] It is headquartered at Tenhipalam, about 20 km (12 mi) south of the city of Calicut, in Malappuram District.
